FINANCIAL INCLUSION

Keystone Bank has developed a financial inclusive strategy which extends our financial services to the disadvantaged and low-income earners across the country through our digital platforms, making banking easier, more convenient and affordable.

KEYSERV AGENCY BANKING

Keyserv is Keystone Bank’s partnership with agents across the country to make banking more accessible, convenient and affordable for the un-banked and under-banked population in remote communities by employing the services of trusted 3rd party retail businesses. Our agent banking ‘outlets’ serve as proxy banking channels where the un-banked or under-served persons carry out banking transactions. These outlets are owned by agents (individual or business entity) who will offer financial services to the customer and in return earn transactional income on the services they rendered. Benefits include - Account opening, Cash deposit, Cash withdrawal, Funds transfer, 24/7 access to your funds, Bills payment and much more,

The Keystone Bank Point of Sale (POS) terminal is a mobile device that enables cardholders make payments for goods and services without the use of physical cash.
Features and Benefits include:

  • It is deployed at no cost to merchants.
  • It operates via GPRS technology on the bundled SIM card or via LAN (local area network) - provided by the Telecoms / Switching companies.
  • It is a collection tool for assisting our customers to collect payments from their clients in a safe and cash free atmosphere.
  • Chip & PIN protected.
  • It allows for payments of goods and services via foreign & Local Debit /Credit/Pre-paid Cards (Verve, Visa, MasterCard).
  • Prints out receipt as evidence of payment.
