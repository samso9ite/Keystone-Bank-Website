Personal Account

Keystone Bank provides a variety of innovative products and services to meet your personal lifestyle needs plus helpful expertise to make your banking experience better and your financial goals for the future more attainable.
From the convenience of mobile and online banking to enjoyable one-on-one discussions and seamless ATM transactions worldwide, we take pride in understanding our customers, tailoring our packages to evolve with their changing needs and growing with them through every phase of life: a child’s first savings account, personal/business achievements and after retirement.
Personal Banking provides useful channels and resources for individuals, limited liability companies, sole proprietorships, registered business names, partnerships, non-governmental associations, clubs, associations, religious bodies and government parastatals, agencies and ministries.

Personal Savings Account

The Keystone Bank Savings account is available for individuals to carry out day-to-day banking transactions seamlessly and conveniently.

With Keystone Bank Savings account banking can be done anytime and anywhere.

Features
  • Minimum opening balance and operating of N500
  • Interest rate of 1.25% per annum (Terms and conditions apply)
  • Issuance of Verve and MasterCard debit cards
  • No restriction to number of withdrawals
  • Access to e-banking services e.g. Mobile Banking and Internet Banking on request
  • Alert notifications by SMS or email through our Transaction Notification Service
  • Cash withdrawals nationwide on about 300 Keystone Bank ATMs and other bank ATMs using the Keystone Bank Debit Card
Requirements
  • Duly filled and signed account opening form
  • Valid means of identification
  • Documentary evidence of address e.g Utility bill
  • BVN
  • Passport photograph
  • Mandate Card
  • Copy of valid ID of the signatory

Quick Save Account

This is a savings account specifically designed for customers with minimal documentation.
The account is aimed at providing financial services to the unbanked and underbanked segment.

Features
  • No opening balance
  • Interest rate of 1.25per annum (terms and conditions applies)
  • No monthly maintenance fee
  • Access to e-banking products – Debit cards and USSD
  • Maximum single deposit of N50,000
  • Maximum cumulative balance of N300,000
  • Account available to Nigerians only
Requirements
  • Duly completed account opening form
  • One Passport photograph
  • Bank Verification Number (BVN) from customer

Quick Save Plus Account

This serves as a higher variant of the Quick Save account, targeted at financially excluded persons.
The customer is, however, required to provide a valid form of identification.

Features
  • No opening balance
  • Interest rate of 1.25% per annum (terms and conditions applies)
  • No monthly maintenance fee
  • Access to e-banking products – Debit cards and USSD
  • Maximum single deposit of N100,000
  • Maximum cumulative balance of N500,000
  • Account available to Nigerians only
Requirements
  • Duly completed account opening form
  • One Passport photograph
  • Verifiable means of identification
  • Bank Verification Number (BVN) from custom

Future Account

Keystone Bank Future Account is a savings account for kids and teenagers designed for parents/guardians to plan for the education of their children or wards and also inculcating a savings culture amongst children. The Future account comes in 2 variants:
Future Kids: age 0 – 10 years
Future Teens: age 11 – 17 years

Features
  • Account can be opened for the child by parents, family friend, NGO, etc.
  • Opening and minimum operating balance of ₦500
  • Interest rate of 1.25% per annum (Terms and conditions applies)
  • Ease of savings through Standing Instructions from parents/guardians account
  • Access to E banking products - Cards, USSD, Mobile banking & Internet banking
  • Cheques and dividend warrants can be paid into the account
  • Safe and secure opportunity to build funds for future use
Requirements
  • Duly filled and signed account opening form
  • Valid means of identification
  • Documentary evidence of address e.g Utility bill
  • BVN of Parents/Guardians
  • Visitation report and KYC form
  • Passport photograph of Child/Ward and Parents/Guardians
  • Mandate Card
  • Copy of valid ID of the signatory

Savings Account Xtra

Savings Account Xtra is a savings account that functions like a current account. The product is designed for Individuals, Businesses, Clubs & Associations, Religious organizations, Cooperatives and NGOs. Find below features & benefits:

Features
  • Opening balance of N5,000 individuals, N10,000 for businesses.
  • Minimum balance of N5,000 individuals, N10,000 for businesses.
  • Interest rate on 1.25% per annum. (Terms and conditions apply)
  • Zero maintenance fee
  • Issuance of Verve and MasterCard debit cards
  • Issuance of non-clearing cheque booklets
  • Clearing cheques & dividend warrants lodgment into account.
  • Third party withdrawals using non-clearing cheque books.
  • Access to E banking products - Cards, USSD, Mobile banking & Internet banking

  • Valid means of identification

  • Documentary evidence of address

  • Passport photograph of each signatory

  • Tax Identification Number

  • BVN from signatories

  • 2 references (corporate entities only)

NIGERIANS IN THE DIASPORA ACCOUNT (NIDA)

Keystone Bank provides the trusted keys to connect with your homeland through the Keystone Nigerians in the Diaspora Account (NIDA). For the first time in Nigeria, you can open and transact with an easy-to-use, seamless bank account from anywhere in the world. All correspondence is done online for your convenience. It comes in Current and Savings account variants

Diaspora Savings Account Xtra
  • Hybrid account (Current and Savings features).
  • Account denominated in Naira, Pounds, Dollar and Euro.
  • Minimum operating balance (USD100, GBP100, EURO 100 & NGN 5,000).
  • Credit interest of 0.15% p.a payable monthly for foreign currency accounts and 1.25% p.a payable monthly on Naira accounts.
  • No maintenance fee
  • Account can be opened as a joint account.
  • Accepts third party cheques (for naira denominated accounts only)
  • Access to Internet Banking and Mobile Banking.
  • Access to third party withdrawals.
  • Access to VISA cards for foreign denominated accounts and MasterCard for Naira denominated accounts.
  • Bills Payment and standing order instructions available
  • Access to the Keystone market place as a Merchant.
  • International transfer available.
  • Telex charge of $25 or its equivalent in Pounds or Euro (for international transfers)
  • 0.5% transfer charges (for international transfers).
Diaspora Current Account
Diaspora Current Account Requirements
  • Duly filled and signed account opening form
  • Notarized or sighted copy of proof of Identification (Scanned Data Page of Nigerian International Passport, NIN/NIMC Card, INEC Voter’s card or Driver’s License)
  • Notarized copy of proof of address (Scanned copy of Utility Bill, Rent Receipt, Credit Card Bill, Driver’s License, Bank Statement or Residence Permit)
  • Scanned Passport Photograph
  • Scanned Signature
  • Two completed reference form from Nigerian banks (For Current account only)
