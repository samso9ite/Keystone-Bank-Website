Corporate Current Account

This is a current account designed for registered business to carry out day-to-day financial activities.

Features
  • Minimum opening balance of N10,000
  • No minimum operating balance
  • Current account maintenance fee of N1 for every N1,000 debit transaction
  • Issuance of cheque booklet valid for clearing
  • Account can be used to access loans
  • No restriction to number of withdrawals

Creative Industry Financing Initiative


If you play in any of the listed industries below, then the Creative Industry Financing Initiative (CIFI) will interest you a lot.

  • Fashion
  • Information Technology
  • Movie
  • Music
CIFI was introduced by the CBN in collaboration with the Bankers’ Committee to improve access to long-term and low interest financing (up to 10 years tenure and an interest rate of 9%) for entrepreneurs and investors in the Nigerian creative and Information Technology sub-sectors. This was in realization of the role the identified sectors would play to promote job creation, reduce poverty and facilitate inclusive growth. CIFI can be accessed at Keystone Bank Limited.

Overview Of Benefits
  • Up to N500 Million for your creative business.
  • Low Interest of 9% per annum
  • Flexible repayment plan spread over 10 years
Requirements
  • Your business must be registered with Corporate Affairs Commission
  • You must have a Business Proposal
  • Your business must have a clean Credit Bureau Report with zero overdue obligations.
  • Your business must be in the creative industry
  • Your business must have a satisfactory CRMS report of owners, sponsors and directors.
How to Apply

Send your business proposal and other supporting documents for your enterprise via cifi@keystonebankng.com and we will get back to you immediately.

We Deliver Customer-focused Trade Finance Solutions. At Keystone Bank, we understand that no two businesses are the same. We provide services to fit dynamic trade finance needs and unique backgrounds throughout the trade cycle.

Differentiation of Trade Instruments
Our customers' instruments requirements are differentiated according to instrument size, duration, payment structure, rating of confirming and advising banks, as well as the relationship between our customer and the beneficiary.

Complexity of the Trade Space
We are aware that the Trade Finance space may seem complicated for customers who are more focused on their Manufacturing/Commodities/Aviation and Agricultural business. Keystone Bank, therefore, collaborates with stakeholders including appointed brokers and representatives of these customers to best arrive at a dynamic and suitable financing solution.

  • Bank Guarantee (BG)
  • Documentary Letter of Credit (LC)
  • Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)
  • Documentary Collections.
  • Trade Advisory Services and Intermediations.
  • Global Coverage
  • Export Receivable Discounting Facility-ERDF
  • Import Finance Facility-IFF
Keystone Bank maintains a global footprint through a strong correspondent banking network and key strategic alliances with multilateral agencies and development finance institutions.

Risk Mitigation
We are a compliant organisation with strict adherence to all KYC/AML policies and procedures in trade. All Letters of Credit issued and advised by Keystone Bank follow the rules pursuant to UCP 600, URC 522, URDG 758 or ISP 98.
