01

CORPORATE CURRENT ACCOUNT

This serves as a higher variant of the Quick Save account, targeted at financially excluded persons. The customer is, however, required to provide a valid form of identification.

Features

  • Maximum single deposit: ₦50,000.00
  • Maximum cumulative deposit: ₦400,000 at any point in time
  • Mobile Banking or Funds Transfer daily limit: ₦100,000.00
  • Zero opening balance
  • Interest Rate of 3.6 %
Steps Required
  • Fill and complete the online Account Opening Form (AOF)
  • Upload your passport photo
  • Visit your branch with a valid ID or Third-Party ID
  • Sign the AOF
02

DOM EXTRA ACCOUNT

Keystone Bank Domiciliary Extra Account is a foreign currency denominated account that is targeted at Limited Liability Companies.

Product Features & Benefits:
  • Account can be denominated in Dollars, Pounds or Euros.
  • Minimum opening balance of 2000 Dollars, Euros or 1000 Pounds.
  • Cash & wire transfers to and from the account (Terms & Conditions Apply).
  • Competitive interest rates.
  • Access to safe and secure E-Banking services & Channels - Mobile Banking, Internet Banking & Debit Cards (Visa, Verve or MasterCard).

For details, kindly contact us at Keystone Bank Limited. 1 Keystone Bank Crescent, Victoria Island, Lagos.0700 2000 3000 or email us at contactcentre@keystonebankng.com.

AGRI BUSINESS

Agric Finance Group offers professional support to all market facing staff of the Bank in the creation of good quality risk asset in all the areas captured within the Agriculture Value Chain. Environmental and Social risk considerations will be undertaken for finance provided anywhere along this value chain. The Group advises both customers and RMs on the best loan structure and farm practices to make the business relationship successful and profitable. The Group gets majority of its lending fund from CBN at a maximum cost of 2% p.a. for on-lending to agribusinesses at a concessionary rate of 9% p.a.
